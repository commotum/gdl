# 5-SCHEDULER

## Result

- The single worker continues down the newly revealed ancestor within a
  configurable fairness quantum, then yields to eligible shallower/high-impact
  work.
- Delayed targets are skipped, shared ancestors are logical singletons, and
  cycle/max-depth boundaries become explicit manual-review work.
- Every network command requires a positive `--max-posts`; no unbounded default
  exists.

## Evidence

- Chain-first, fairness-yield, shared target, retry eligibility, cycle, depth,
  and bounded-worker tests pass.
